## 1. CUSTOMER SERVICE AI (Klarna-Style)

### 1.1 Klarna
| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Use Case** | AI customer service assistant handling full journey: queries, refunds, disputes, support |
| **Industry** | Fintech (buy-now-pay-later) |
| **Results** | $40M annual savings (early 2024); $60M by Q3 2025; 2.3M conversations/month; 700–853 FTE equivalent; 82% faster resolution (11 min → <2 min); 2/3 of chats resolved by AI; CSAT parity with humans (75% → 90% in some reports) |
| **Implementation** | Partnership with OpenAI; deployed across 35+ markets |
| **Tech Stack** | OpenAI-powered assistant |
| **Sales Angle** | Scale support without linear cost growth; handle millions of inquiries across many languages; reduce resolution time and maintain satisfaction |
| **Evidence** | [AI Traction](https://www.ai-traction.com/en-US/insights/klarna-ai-case-study), [Spearhead](https://spearhead.so/klarnas-ai-delivers-700x-efficiency-targets-40-million-profit-boost-in-2024/), [CX Dive](https://www.customerexperiencedive.com/news/klarna-says-ai-agent-work-853-employees/805987/) |

|---------|---------------|------------------------|
| Support costs scaling with volume | Automate routine queries; maintain CSAT | 25–70% cost reduction; payback 1–6 months |

| Call center costs and attrition | Voice AI; 24/7 coverage | 30–70% cost reduction; 391% ROI (PolyAI) |

| Legal/healthcare compliance and cost | Specialized AI for research and docs | 284% ROI (legal); 451% ROI (healthcare) |
